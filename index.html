Riding Guardians Twice 🚀
Strategy to ride Guardians a second time
Guardians is LL Single only — you can't book it again through LL Multi. Here's how to get a second ride:
1. Virtual Queue (7 AM & 1 PM) — Disney sometimes offers a free virtual queue boarding group. Check the My Disney Experience app at exactly 7:00 AM and 1:00 PM. If available, snag a spot immediately — they go in seconds.
2. Buy a second LL Single — After you use your 4:45 PM LL Single, check if additional LL Single passes are still available to purchase for later in the evening. Pricing is per person.
3. Standby at park close — In the last 45 min before 9 PM, standby lines often drop significantly. Head to Guardians at ~8:15 PM and check the posted wait time.
Pro Tips
⚡
Book next LL immediately
After tapping into each LL Multi Pass ride, book your next one right away in the app. You can hold one LL at a time (or one every 2 hrs).
🌅
Rope Drop = No Lines
At 9 AM rope drop, Remy's line is often under 20 min. By 10:30 AM the same ride can be 60+ min.
🧒
Maverick (age 5) note
Maverick meets all height requirements for your booked rides. Confirm at the gate — cast members measure at 40", 42", 44".
🗺
World Showcase opens 11 AM
Don't head to Norway or France before 11 AM — shops and most pavilions don't open until then.
🌧
Afternoon storms possible
March in Florida: afternoon showers are common but brief. Duck into a ride or pavilion — lines actually get shorter during rain.
💧
Free water refills
Any quick-service location will give you free cups of water. Stay hydrated — March can still be warm.
